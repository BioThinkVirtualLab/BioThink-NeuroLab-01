Interactive labs for thinking like a neuroscientist.

Welcome to the BioThink Virtual Neuroscience Lab. These simulations connect core neuroscience concepts to sensory processing, brain-computer interfaces, artificial intelligence, scientific literature, and the review article we will build together.

Foundations of Neuroscience

Explore how neurons generate signals, communicate across synapses, and carry sensory information through the nervous system.

Lab 01

Membrane & Action Potential Lab

Control Na⁺ and K⁺ channels and explore resting potential, threshold, depolarization, repolarization, hyperpolarization, and recovery.

Neuron Firing Simulator

Touch Perception Simulation
